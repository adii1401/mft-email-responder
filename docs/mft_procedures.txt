MFT SUPPORT - STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES
============================================================

1. PASSWORD RESET POLICY
   - Password reset requests must come from authorized Trading Partner (TP) contacts only.
   - Job Owner (JO) approval is MANDATORY before processing any password reset.
   - Always CC the Job Owner in the reply email.
   - Confirm TP identity before resetting credentials.

2. CONNECTION FAILURE TROUBLESHOOTING
   - Check MFT logs for error codes.
   - Verify firewall/IP whitelist settings with TP.
   - Confirm protocol settings: SFTP (port 22), FTPS (port 21/990), AS2 (port 4080).
   - Escalate to L2 if unresolved within 2 hours.

3. TRANSFER FAILURE POLICY
   - Acknowledge email within 1 hour.
   - Check BIS Process Monitor for transfer status.
   - Notify JO if business-critical file is delayed by more than 30 minutes.

4. NEW TP ONBOARDING
   - Requires JO sign-off before initiating setup.
   - Setup time: 3-5 business days.
   - Provide TP with connection details only after JO approval.

5. ESCALATION MATRIX
   - L1 Support: Initial triage, basic troubleshooting (0-2 hrs)
   - L2 Support: Complex issues, firewall changes (2-4 hrs)
   - JO: Business escalations, approvals
   - Manager: SLA breaches
